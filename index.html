Solar System

The solar system is a gravitationally bound system. From the objects that orbit the Sun directly, there are two main categories: Inner Planets and Outer Planets. The four smaller inner planets, Mercury, Venus, Earth and Mars are terrestrial planets. In the outer planets there are the two largest planets, Jupiter and Saturn, gas giants, and the two outermost plantes Uranus and Neptune, which are ice giants.
